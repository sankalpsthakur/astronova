# Pattern Library

A pattern is a recurring loop between stimulus, automatic script, and outcome. Each one is wired to specific chart structures — Western placements and Vedic bhavas. When current transits or active dashas energize those structures, the loop fires faster and harder. Astronova surfaces the pattern that is most activated right now so you can intercept it before the default script runs.

---

## Recognition Threat
**Activation:** High
**Western drivers:** Mars (primary), Sun (ego), 10H (status)
**Vedic drivers:** Mangal (primary), Surya, 10th bhava, current Mangal dasha/antardasha amplifies
**Detection cues:**
- Mars in 10H, 1H, or conjunct Sun within 5°
- Sun-Mars aspect (square, opposition, conjunction)
- Mangal mahadasha or antardasha active, or Mangal in 10th bhava

### The Loop
| Stage | Content |
|---|---|
| Stimulus | Your competence is questioned in front of people who matter. |
| Default Script | Counter-attack. Prove the questioner wrong. Escalate volume or precision. |
| Hidden Need | To be seen as capable and respected by this specific audience. |
| Low Consciousness Output | You win the argument and lose the relationship. Status drops anyway. |
| High Consciousness Route | Pause 7 seconds. Separate the critique from the critic. Ask one clarifying question. |
| Optimal Action | "Let me clarify the actual issue before I respond." |

### Body Signature
Heat in the face and chest within two seconds. Jaw and shoulders lock. Breath shortens high in the ribs.

### Activation Score Inputs
- Mars transit to natal Sun, Mars, or 10H ruler (orb under 3°)
- Saturn transit squaring or opposing natal Mars
- Active Mangal antardasha within a Surya or Shani mahadasha

## Control Under Uncertainty
**Activation:** High
**Western drivers:** Saturn (primary), Mercury (planning), 6H (routine), 10H (outcome)
**Vedic drivers:** Shani (primary), Budha, 6th and 10th bhavas, current Shani dasha/antardasha amplifies
**Detection cues:**
- Saturn in 1H, 6H, or 10H, or aspecting the Moon
- Mercury-Saturn aspect (conjunction, square, opposition)
- Shani mahadasha active, or Shani in kendra (1, 4, 7, 10) bhavas

### The Loop
| Stage | Content |
|---|---|
| Stimulus | A plan you depend on develops a variable you cannot predict. |
| Default Script | Over-prepare. Build redundant systems. Demand certainty from people who do not have it. |
| Hidden Need | A felt sense of safety before action is allowed to begin. |
| Low Consciousness Output | Decision paralysis. The window closes while you are still planning. |
| High Consciousness Route | One breath. Name the smallest reversible action and do only that within 60 seconds. |
| Optimal Action | "I will do the next 10-minute step and re-decide after that." |

### Body Signature
Cold tightness in the gut. Eyes narrow, scanning. Hands grip whatever they are near.

### Activation Score Inputs
- Saturn transit to natal Moon, Mercury, or ascendant (orb under 5°)
- Stationary Mercury or Mercury retrograde squaring natal Saturn
- Shani sade sati or ashtama shani phase active

## Solitude Recharge
**Activation:** Medium
**Western drivers:** Moon (primary), 12H (retreat), 4H (private base)
**Vedic drivers:** Chandra (primary), 12th and 4th bhavas, current Chandra dasha amplifies
**Detection cues:**
- Moon in 12H, 4H, or in Cancer/Pisces
- Moon-Neptune or Moon-Saturn aspect
- Chandra mahadasha or antardasha active, or Chandra in 12th bhava

### The Loop
| Stage | Content |
|---|---|
| Stimulus | A full day of input — meetings, messages, ambient social demand. |
| Default Script | Push through. Stay available. Treat the need for solitude as weakness. |
| Hidden Need | Unbroken time alone to metabolize input and reset baseline. |
| Low Consciousness Output | You stay reachable, get nothing absorbed, and wake up irritable. |
| High Consciousness Route | Block 30 minutes. No phone. No music with lyrics. Single window of stillness. |
| Optimal Action | "I am offline until 4 — text me only if it is on fire." |

### Body Signature
Heaviness behind the eyes. The skin feels thin. Voices sound louder than they are.

### Activation Score Inputs
- Moon transit through natal 12H or 4H
- Full Moon or eclipse within 7 days of natal Moon
- Chandra antardasha inside a Shani or Rahu mahadasha

## Fast Execution / Slow Recovery
**Activation:** Medium
**Western drivers:** Mars (execution), Mercury (speed), 6H (work cycle), 8H (recovery)
**Vedic drivers:** Mangal, Budha, 6th and 8th bhavas, current Mangal or Budha dasha amplifies
**Detection cues:**
- Mars-Mercury conjunction or trine
- Mars in 3H or 6H
- Mangal-Budha yoga, or Mangal in upachaya bhavas (3, 6, 10, 11)

### The Loop
| Stage | Content |
|---|---|
| Stimulus | A clear task with a deadline you respect. |
| Default Script | Sprint to completion. Skip food, sleep, and transition time. Queue the next task immediately. |
| Hidden Need | A nervous system that has actually returned to baseline before the next push. |
| Low Consciousness Output | Three days of brilliance, then a week of foggy underperformance you blame on yourself. |
| High Consciousness Route | After ship, wait 24 hours before committing to the next sprint. No exceptions. |
| Optimal Action | "I shipped. The next decision happens tomorrow, not now." |

### Body Signature
A wired buzz in the limbs after delivery. Sleep is shallow. Appetite is off — either ravenous or absent.

### Activation Score Inputs
- Mars transit conjunct or trine natal Mercury
- Jupiter transit to natal Mars (amplifies output, masks fatigue)
- Mangal antardasha inside a Budha or Guru mahadasha

## Emotional Delayed Processing
**Activation:** Medium
**Western drivers:** Moon (primary), Mercury (articulation), 4H (interior), 8H (depth)
**Vedic drivers:** Chandra (primary), Budha, 4th and 8th bhavas, current Chandra or Budha dasha amplifies
**Detection cues:**
- Moon-Mercury hard aspect (square, opposition), or Moon in 8H
- Mercury in 12H, or Mercury combust by Sun
- Chandra in 8th bhava, or Budha aspected by Shani

### The Loop
| Stage | Content |
|---|---|
| Stimulus | Something lands emotionally — a comment, a loss, an unexpected kindness. |
| Default Script | File it. Stay functional. Respond with logistics, not feeling. |
| Hidden Need | Time and language to know what you actually feel before you have to perform a reaction. |
| Low Consciousness Output | The feeling surfaces three days later, attached to a wrong target — someone trivial gets the full charge. |
| High Consciousness Route | Wait 24 hours before any reply that is not strictly logistical. Write the feeling down once. |
| Optimal Action | "I will get back to you on this tomorrow — I want to think clearly first." |

### Body Signature
A small delay between hearing words and feeling them. Throat tightens hours later. Dreams get vivid.

### Activation Score Inputs
- Moon transit to natal Mercury or 8H cusp
- Mercury retrograde over natal Moon
- Chandra antardasha inside a Budha mahadasha, or Shani drishti on natal Chandra
