# Consciousness Levels

Levels are not fixed traits. They shift with awareness, sleep, stress, and practice. The same person operates at different levels for different planets — Level 4 Mercury and Level 1 Venus can live in one body on the same afternoon. Movement is bidirectional. A trigger drops you down. A pause moves you up. The ladder is a mirror, not a verdict.

---

## Sun / Surya
**Western theme:** Identity, vitality, will, the core self
**Vedic theme:** Surya — soul, father, authority, dignity; the karaka of atma (self)

| Level | Name | Keywords | Body cue | One-line behavior |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Reactive Sun | Ego, pride, performance, demand | Puffed chest, raised chin, tight throat | Needs to be seen; collapses when overlooked |
| 2 | Productive Sun | Confidence, ambition, presence, ownership | Upright posture, steady eye contact, full breath | Takes the stage; signs his name to the work |
| 3 | Strategic Sun | Sovereign authority, clarity of purpose, dignity | Centered weight, calm gaze, unhurried speech | Leads without needing applause; chooses what to bless |
| 4 | Mastered Sun | Light without strain, generative presence, quiet kingship | Soft eyes, steady breath, hands at rest | Warms a room by entering it; gives credit and keeps the throne |

**Growth edge (Level 2 → 3):** Stop performing for the audience that already chose you.
**Growth edge (Level 3 → 4):** Lead from the body, not the title — let presence do the work.
**Common regression trigger:** Public dismissal, being overlooked, status threat.
