# Pause Layer — Emotion → Planet Protocols

Tap the emotion. Run the protocol. 30 to 90 seconds. Then act.

---

## Anger → Mars Protocol
**Vedic correspondence:** Mangal — element fire (agni), direction south, day Tuesday, mantra "Om Angarakaya Namaha"
**The energy is for:** Cutting through what no longer serves; defending what matters.

### Step 1 — Name (5 seconds)
"I am feeling anger." Say it out loud or in your head. Do not justify it. Do not story it.

### Step 2 — Locate (10 seconds)
Where is it in your body?
- Heat in face, jaw, throat
- Tightness in chest, fists
- Pressure behind the eyes

Put a hand on the strongest location.

### Step 3 — Breathe (15 seconds)
Mars breath: inhale 4 counts through the nose. Hold 2. Exhale 6 through the mouth with sound (audible "haaa"). Three rounds.

### Step 4 — Reframe (10 seconds)
This is fuel, not fault. Anger means a boundary was crossed or a value was violated. The question is not "how do I stop being angry" — it's "what is this anger asking me to protect?"

### Step 5 — Route (30 seconds)
Pick one:
- **If at someone:** Ask the clarifying question. "Help me understand what you meant by ___."
- **If at a situation:** Write the boundary in one sentence. "From now on, I do not ___."
- **If at yourself:** Identify the unmet need. "What did I want that I didn't get?"

### Step 6 — Optional ritual (Vedic)
Light a red candle. Or splash cold water on wrists. Or do 10 push-ups. Mars wants discharge through the body, not the mouth.

### Do NOT
- Send the text.
- Make the decision.
- Speak in the meeting.

For the next 7 minutes.

## Sadness → Moon Protocol
**Vedic correspondence:** Chandra — element water (jala), direction northwest, day Monday, mantra "Om Chandraya Namaha"
**The energy is for:** Receiving, releasing, integrating what the day did not let you feel.

### Step 1 — Name (5 seconds)
"I am feeling sadness." Say it. Do not minimize it. Do not call it tired, fine, or off.

### Step 2 — Locate (10 seconds)
Where is it in your body?
- Heaviness behind the eyes, throat
- Hollow in the chest or stomach
- Weight in the shoulders

Put a hand on the strongest location. Let it rest there.

### Step 3 — Breathe (20 seconds)
Moon breath: inhale 6 counts through the nose. Exhale 8 counts through the nose, longer than the inhale. Soft, no force. Two rounds.

### Step 4 — Reframe (10 seconds)
Sadness is the body metabolizing a loss — small or large, named or unnamed. It is not a problem to fix. It is water passing through. Your only job is to let it pass.

### Step 5 — Route (30 seconds)
Pick one:
- **Somatic release:** Let the tears come if they are there. One long sigh. A slow 5-minute walk, no phone.
- **Water:** Drink a full glass slowly. Or splash your face. Or step into the shower. If you can reach an ocean, lake, or river — go.
- **Name the loss:** One sentence. "I am sad because ___ is not what I wanted it to be."

### Step 6 — Optional ritual (Vedic)
Light a white or silver candle. Place a bowl of water near you. Look at the moon if it is night. Chandra does not ask you to be okay — only to be present with what is.

### Do NOT
- Scroll.
- Numb.
- Call the person you broke up with.

For the next 20 minutes.

## Fear → Saturn Protocol
**Vedic correspondence:** Shani — element earth and air, direction west, day Saturday, mantra "Om Shanaye Namaha"
**The energy is for:** Structural caution; respect for limits; long-term wisdom that the lizard brain mistakes for danger now.

### Step 1 — Name (5 seconds)
"I am feeling fear." Not anxious. Not stressed. Fear. The precise word is the first weight off.

### Step 2 — Locate (10 seconds)
Where is it in your body?
- Tightness in the gut, shallow breath
- Buzzing in the chest, racing heart
- Cold hands, locked shoulders

Put a hand on the strongest location. Feet flat on the floor.

### Step 3 — Breathe (20 seconds)
Saturn breath: box breath. Inhale 4. Hold 4. Exhale 4. Hold 4. Two rounds. Mouth closed throughout.

### Step 4 — Reframe (10 seconds)
Fear is your nervous system asking for structure, not escape. It is information about a future you have not built scaffolding for yet. The task is to give it scaffolding.

### Step 5 — Route (30 seconds)
Two sentences, written down. Phone notes, paper, whatever is closest.
1. **Worst case:** "The worst that can happen is ___." Specific. One sentence.
2. **Verifiable next:** "In the next 24 hours I can verify ___." One concrete check — a message, a search, a number, a person.

If you cannot verify anything in 24 hours, the fear is about something further out. Schedule a 15-minute block this week to plan it. Then close the loop.

### Step 6 — Optional ritual (Vedic)
Light a dark blue or black candle. Touch the earth — barefoot on floor, hand on stone, palm on a tree. Shani is slow on purpose. He is asking you to slow down with him.

### Do NOT
- Catastrophize on the phone.
- Make the irreversible choice.
- Refresh the page.

For the next 30 minutes.

## Desire → Venus Protocol
**Vedic correspondence:** Shukra — element water (jala), direction southeast, day Friday, mantra "Om Shukraya Namaha"
**The energy is for:** Pointing at what is meaningful; calibrating your values; showing you where you want to grow toward.

### Step 1 — Name (5 seconds)
"I am feeling desire." Or wanting. Or pull. Name the object: "I want ___." Out loud is better.

### Step 2 — Locate (10 seconds)
Where is it in your body?
- Warmth in the chest, heart racing
- Pull in the lower belly, the hips
- Lightness in the head, restlessness

Put a hand on the chest, palm flat.

### Step 3 — Breathe (20 seconds)
Heart breath: hand on chest. Inhale 5 through the nose, into the hand. Exhale 5 through the nose. Slow, even. Three rounds. Let the hand feel the rise and fall.

### Step 4 — Reframe (10 seconds)
Desire is a compass needle, not a command. It points. It does not say "now." The signal matters. The timing is yours.

### Step 5 — Route (30 seconds)
Distinguish craving from longing.
- **Craving** is away from something — a feeling you do not want to have. Boredom, loneliness, smallness. The object is incidental.
- **Longing** is toward something — a quality of life you want more of. Beauty, intimacy, mastery, meaning. The object is a doorway.

Write one sentence: "This is craving / longing because ___."

Then: **wait 24 hours before acting.** If at hour 24 it is still there, it is longing. Act with care. If it dissolved, it was craving. Notice what it was actually pointing at.

### Step 6 — Optional ritual (Vedic)
Light a white or pink candle. Eat something sweet, slowly. Look at something beautiful for 60 seconds — art, a face, a flower, the sky. Shukra teaches that beauty is not optional; it is how the soul calibrates.

### Do NOT
- Send the message.
- Buy the thing.
- Open the app.

For the next 24 hours.

## Confusion → Mercury Protocol
**Vedic correspondence:** Budha — element earth and air, direction north, day Wednesday, mantra "Om Budhaya Namaha"
**The energy is for:** Information that has not sorted yet. The mind is mid-process, not broken.

### Step 1 — Name (5 seconds)
"I am feeling confusion." Not stupid. Not behind. Confused. The data is in; the pattern is not.

### Step 2 — Locate (10 seconds)
Where is it in your body?
- Static behind the eyes, fog in the forehead
- Tightness across the temples
- Restlessness in the hands, the jaw

Put a hand on the forehead or the back of the neck.

### Step 3 — Breathe (20 seconds)
Mercury breath: equal breath. Inhale 4 through the nose. Exhale 4 through the nose. Mouth closed. Four rounds. Eyes soft or closed.

### Step 4 — Reframe (10 seconds)
Confusion is not the absence of thought. It is too much thought, unsorted. The fix is not more input. The fix is structure.

### Step 5 — Route (30 seconds)
Three sentences. Write them down.
1. **What I know:** One thing that is certain.
2. **What I don't know:** One thing that is genuinely unclear.
3. **What I'd need to know to decide:** One specific piece of information.

If the third sentence is answerable — go find the answer. If it is not, the decision is not ready yet. That is a fact, not a failure.

### Step 6 — Optional ritual (Vedic)
Light a green candle. Pick up a pen and write longhand for two minutes — anything, do not lift the pen. Or take a 10-minute walk without your phone. Budha sorts in motion and in ink, not in the scroll.

### Do NOT
- Ask three more people.
- Open another tab.
- Decide.

For the next 60 minutes.

## Pause. Name it. Route it. Use the energy.

Emotion is data, not enemy. The planet is the energy signature, not the cause — Mars does not make you angry; anger is the shape Mars-energy takes when it moves through a human body. The protocol is your pre-committed response to your own dysregulation: written when calm, run when not. You do not have to be enlightened in the moment. You only have to remember which button to press.
